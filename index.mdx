## Get started in four steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Register at the FitWave home page with your name, email, and password. Once registered, you'll be logged in automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Browse gym classes">
    From your dashboard, explore all available classes — each card shows the class description, difficulty, trainer, and all available schedule slots.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick a schedule and book">
    Select a class, choose a time slot from the weekly schedule, and confirm your booking. You'll see it appear in **My Upcoming Classes** immediately.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Manage your bookings">
    View all your reservations on your dashboard. Cancel any upcoming booking with a single click if your plans change.
  </Step>
</Steps>
